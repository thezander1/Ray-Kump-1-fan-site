About Ray Kump

Ray Kump is a comedian, writer, and co-host of the popular podcast "The Tim Dillon Show." Known for his sharp wit, eccentric personality, and unmatched ability to riff on politics and conspiracy culture, Ray has built a loyal fanbase that admires his fearless humor and insights.

He has also hosted his own solo projects, including "Kump," where his unfiltered takes and offbeat commentary have cemented him as a unique voice in modern comedy.

Podcast Highlights

Ray rose to prominence alongside Tim Dillon, delivering countless unforgettable podcast moments. From dissecting bizarre internet subcultures to lampooning elite power structures, Ray's contributions to the show helped define its irreverent tone.
