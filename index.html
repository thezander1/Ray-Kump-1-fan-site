About Ray Kump

Ray Kump is an American comedian and podcaster known for his sharp wit, absurdist humor, and unfiltered takes on politics, economics, and urban decay. As the host of the Kump Podcast, Ray distills his unique life experiences—from time in prison to working at a morgue—into hilarious and chaotic commentary alongside co-host Lucie Steiner.

A frequent collaborator with comedian Tim Dillon, Ray has co-hosted The Tim Dillon Show (formerly Tim Dillon Is Going to Hell) and appeared in over 45 episodes. His other credits include guest spots on Real Ass Podcast, Social Villains, and Unnecessary Robots, as well as acting roles in Cherry Bomb (2018) and The 2016 Chris Gethard Election Special.

With an estimated net worth of $1 million, Ray has built a loyal fanbase through his raw, authentic comedy and engaging podcast presence. Whether riffing on current events or diving into wild hypotheticals like “100 men vs. a gorilla,” Ray Kump delivers laughs with a side of social critique.
