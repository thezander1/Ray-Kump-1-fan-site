About the Show

Kump Podcast is the solo project of comedian Ray Kump, formerly of the cult-favorite podcast Part of the Problem and a one-time co-creator of the unhinged chaos that was Going to Hell. Since going solo, Kump has leaned into his absurdist worldview, satirical fury, and niche obsessions—from medieval torture devices to international smuggling routes.

Kump doesn’t give you what you want; he gives you what you didn’t know you needed. And sometimes that’s an eight-minute breakdown of how long meat can sit unrefrigerated in a Dodge Dart.
